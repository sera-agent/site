# localai

**Affordable AI inference hardware for local businesses.**

We build and service custom inference servers under €10,000 — giving you private, on-premise AI without cloud dependencies or per-token costs.

---

## What We Offer

- **Pre-built inference servers** — Turnkey hardware ready to run LLMs, image generators, and more
- **Serviced & maintained** — We handle setup, updates, and support
- **Your data stays yours** — Everything runs locally, no cloud transmission

---

## Why Local Inference?

| Cloud AI | Local AI |
|----------|----------|
| Pay per token / subscription | One-time hardware cost |
| Data sent to third parties | Data never leaves your premises |
| Dependent on internet | Works offline |
| Usage limits & throttling | Unlimited inference |
| Privacy concerns | Full compliance control |
